Description

Ge-STAR, Germanium-based Science and Technology Advancement Research, is an NSF-supported statewide research incubator integrating artificial intelligence with high-purity germanium crystal growth and detector development.

The meeting will bring together faculty members, researchers, students, technical staff, and collaborators working in germanium detector technologies, AI-assisted materials research, low-background physics, detector instrumentation, medical imaging, scientific instrumentation, and education.
