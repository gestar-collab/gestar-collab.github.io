Welcome to Ge-STAR

The Ge-STAR (Germanium-Science Technology Advancement Research) collaboration focuses on developing advanced detector simulations and experimental analysis frameworks for cutting-edge nuclear and particle physics experiments.

Our work builds upon the success of the PIRE-GEMADARC initiative and expands international cooperation in detector development, simulation, and scientific outreach.
